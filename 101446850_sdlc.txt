evOps, a combination of "Development" and "Operations," is a significant aspect of the software development cycle. It bridges the gap between development and operations teams, fostering a culture of collaboration and shared responsibility. Learning DevOps engineering is crucial for several reasons.Scalability:* Infrastructure as Code (IaC) is a key DevOps practice that manages and provisions computing infrastructure with machine-readable script files. IaC allows the system to handle increased usage efficiently.Collaboration:* DevOps encourages a culture where developers and operations teams collaborate closely. Shared responsibility and transparency lead to less blame-shifting and more problem-solving
